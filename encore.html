Project Overview
We’ve all been there: wanting to pick up a new instrument, but feeling lost in the process and embarrassed of your skill level. But most of all, you feel alone. Encore! is a music learning community that focuses on including beginners in musical spaces that typically feel exclusive.

Online communities, such as Reddit, are full of information but may cause certain groups to feel excluded by the preexisting culture. Encore! aims to foster a judgment-free culture where people are unafraid to make mistakes for the sake of learning. With Encore!, users can publicly share posts to their community containing their struggles, resources, or relatable content. They can also add to their Learning Diary, a customizable digital log of the user’s musical progress.

Ultimately, Encore will help aspiring musicians ease into a vibrant community without feeling alone and excluded.
